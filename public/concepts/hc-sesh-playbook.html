Strategic Overview

The Sesh is a Cultural Asset.
We treat it like one.

"Every Sesh should generate three things: genuine appreciation for the people in that room, a data asset we own, and content that fuels six weeks of brand presence."

Phase 1 — T-Minus 5–6 Weeks

The Discovery Gate

Finalize the "Core 12" variables across two partner meetings. No external outreach begins until Meeting 2 is signed off.

Meeting 1 — Internal / Partner
Establish the Foundation
  • Lock Event Date & Missouri City
  • Confirm Venue Name, Venue Cost
  • Set Capacity parameters
  • Assign internal Project Owners
Meeting 2 — Partner / Venue
Confirm All Externals
  • Venue Address (Eventbrite/Smartwaiver)
  • Partners, F&B (Pappo's / Sister Cities)
  • Security: 5th Meridian
  • Music talent, Plus-Up costs
◆ Phase 1 Addition — Content & Data Planning
  • Book videographer and photographer at Meeting 1 — these are non-negotiable line items, not afterthoughts.
  • Define the data capture goal: target number of email + phone registrations for this Sesh.
  • Confirm Eventbrite collects email + phone as required fields — verify this before launch in Phase 2.
  • Assign a Content Lead (internal or external) who will own the shot list and post-sesh delivery timeline.
Phase 2 — T-Minus 4 Weeks

Admin & Design Lockdown

Build the digital infrastructure and prep physical kits. The "Big Four" flyers are the visual backbone of the event experience.

Digital Infrastructure
Eventbrite + Smartwaiver
  • Launch Eventbrite with required fields: Name, Email, Phone, Brand/Dispensary
  • Launch Smartwaiver with QR code for check-in
  • Test full registration flow end-to-end
  • Confirm Eventbrite data exports to Google Sheet or CRM
The Big Four Flyers
Design Deliverables
  • Main Event Flyer — Eventbrite / Socials
  • Check-In Flyer — Smartwaiver QR code
  • Dab Bar Menu — specific cultivars / profiles
  • Lucid Rewards Flyer — signup QR
◆ Phase 2 Addition — 5th Flyer: Data Capture CTA
  • Design a "Stay in the Loop" flyer — a dedicated sign-up QR that routes to a branded form (not just Eventbrite) for post-event newsletter + SMS opt-in.
  • The form should capture: First Name, Phone, Email, Dispensary/Brand affiliation, Brand preference (HeadChange / Safe Bet / Bubbles). Use Google Forms, Typeform, or the Curador Intake Portal architecture.
  • Confirm with Postscript/Klaviyo that phone numbers captured via this form can flow into the SMS list automatically.
  • Kit Audit: add the 5th flyer (10 printed units) to the Apache Case 2 manifest.
Phase 3 — T-Minus 2 Weeks

The Inventory Pull

Secure the concentrates and hardware. This phase also finalizes the content crew brief so they arrive knowing exactly what to capture.

Concentrates
The Sample Run
  • Select cultivars for Dab Bar Menu
  • Pickup 3–4 cases (48–64 units) from partner dispensary (e.g., Cookies)
  • Log every gram by strain and batch for Empty Jar Audit
Hardware
Equipment Check
  • 2+ Temp Tech devices — test and charge
  • DC Cobra Coils + Quad Enail Box
  • Terp Sou Vides — charged for thermal floor verification
  • Confirm power supply available at venue for each dab station
◆ Phase 3 Addition — Content Crew Brief
  • Send the videographer and photographer the shot list (see Content Team section) and venue walk-through photos if available.
  • Share the event timeline (load-in through close) so they can plan coverage arcs.
  • Confirm deliverable format: RAW + JPEG for photo, 4K ProRes or H.264 for video, delivered via shared Drive folder within 72 hours post-event.
  • Define social-first vertical cuts needed (9:16 for Reels/TikTok) vs. archival horizontal.
Phase 4 — T-Minus 1 Week

The Final Sprint

Transition from digital planning to physical execution across Monday through Sunday. Every day has a specific focus and owner.

Mon – Tue

Logistics Lockdown

Airbnb / travel confirmed. Security final call with 5th Meridian — guard count (door vs. street parking), 4-hour window. Travel and supply check with full team. Print + laminate all five flyers from Phase 2.

Wed

Concentrate Pull + Plus-Up Fulfillment

Visit Cookies / NatureMED. Secure 3–4 cases of concentrates. Log every gram by strain and batch. Final shopping for Plus-Up supplies (DIY Bead station, Mario Kart prizes, drinks, bonfire materials).

Thu

Apache Case Audit

Open every case — verify all counts. Rig Case 1: 3 Rigs + 1 backup, 6+ Bangers. Production Case 1: 500 wristbands, iPad charged. Production Case 2: 200+ stickers, Lanyards bundled. DC Coil Burn-In test to 550°F.

Fri

Consumable Resupply

2+ gallons 91–99% ISO. 3+ fresh packs Glob Mops / HC Swabs. 4+ packs Alcohol Wipes. 3 gallons distilled water. F&B final sync text to Pappo's Pizza or Sister Cities — confirm delivery address.

Sun

Travel & Prep

Load van in order of use: Production Cases 1 & 2 first (check-in), Rig Case last. Final text to Project Owners (Cory, Ian, Matt) with Airbnb address and Monday Call Time.

◆ Phase 4 Addition — Content Crew Logistics
  • Thursday: Share final venue load-in time and parking instructions with videographer and photographer.
  • Friday: Confirm content crew call time — recommend arriving 30–45 minutes before doors open to capture setup, product styling, and empty-room ambiance shots.
  • Sunday: Add content crew to the staff brief text. Include call time, venue address, and point-of-contact for day-of.
  • Pack product styling kit for hero shots: branded mat, jars, tools, dab bar arrangement. Assign a team member to style each product shot.
Data Strategy

Capturing the Room

Every person in that room is a warm industry contact. We should leave with their information and their permission to stay in touch. This section defines exactly how we collect and activate that data.

Eventbrite Registration — Required Fields

First + Last Name
Standard. Used for check-in list, waiver matching, and personalization.
Email Address
Primary CRM field. Must export to Klaviyo after each event. Tag with: Sesh-[City]-[YYYY-MM]
Phone Number
Required for SMS opt-in. Route to Postscript. Tag: HeadChange-Sesh. Confirm TCPA opt-in language is in Eventbrite copy.
Dispensary / Brand
Custom field. Critical for B2B segmentation — who works where, which accounts to activate.
Role
Dropdown: Budtender / Manager / Brand Rep / Industry / Other. Helps segment post-sesh communications.

On-Site "Stay in the Loop" Capture

Capture Point
QR code on printed flyer + table tent at every dab station and at check-in. Links to a branded Google Form or Typeform (HeadChange color palette).
Form Fields
First Name, Phone, Email, Dispensary/Brand, Fave HeadChange product (optional). Keep it short — under 30 seconds to complete.
The CTA
"Get first access to new drops, exclusive Sesh invites, and HeadChange merch." Value-first language. Not "join our mailing list."
Incentive
Consider offering a small merch item (sticker pack, branded dab mat) for on-site sign-ups. Creates a moment, reinforces brand.
Automation
Form submission triggers: (1) welcome email via Klaviyo within 1 hour. (2) SMS welcome via Postscript. Both reference the Sesh they just attended.
Post-Event Data Hygiene
Clean + Segment Immediately
  • Export Eventbrite list within 24 hours of event close
  • Tag all contacts: Sesh-[City]-[Date] in Klaviyo
  • Identify dispensary clusters (3+ people from same account = priority follow-up)
  • Flag VIPs, glassblowers, industry guests for direct outreach
  • Merge any duplicates between Eventbrite + on-site form
Compliance Notes
Stay Clean, Stay Legal
  • Include clear SMS opt-in language on Eventbrite and on-site form
  • Retain TCPA consent records for all SMS contacts
  • All email sends must include unsubscribe link
  • Do not add Sesh contacts to general consumer lists without re-consent
  • Cannabis marketing compliance varies by state — review before sending to cross-state contacts

Content Crew Deliverables & Timeline

Delivery Window
All files delivered to shared Google Drive folder within 72 hours of event close.
Photo Format
RAW files + exported JPEGs (2400px+ on long edge, sRGB). Organized by shot category folder.
Video Format
4K H.264 or ProRes master files. Pre-cut social verticals (9:16, 15–30 seconds) in a separate subfolder. No music added — deliver clean audio so we can add licensed music.
Selects Call
Photographer delivers a "selects folder" of their top 30–40 edited JPEGs within 72 hours. Full RAW delivery can follow within 7 days.
Usage Rights
Confirm in contract: HeadChange Brands / Curador owns full usage rights for marketing, social, print, and digital. Photographer credited on organic posts.
Post-Event Strategy

Content Recycling & Follow-Through

The Sesh ends at the venue. The value compounds for weeks after. This is the content recycling matrix and engagement sequence that turns one night into six weeks of brand momentum.

T+0 to T+48hrs
The Immediate Window
  • Post 1–2 candid photos to Stories while event is live (assigned to Social Lead)
  • Tag venue, partners, glassblowers immediately for co-shares
  • Send same-night or next-morning "Thank you for coming" SMS via Postscript to all attendees
  • Deploy welcome email sequence in Klaviyo to new list captures
  • Export Eventbrite attendee list + merge with on-site sign-ups
T+3 to T+7 Days
The Core Drop
  • Post photo gallery (3–5 selects) to Instagram feed — caption focuses on community, not product
  • Post Reels cut from event video — dab bar action, crowd energy, testimonials
  • Send post-sesh email to attendee list: recap, photo gallery link, what's next
  • Direct DMs or personal text to identified VIPs, dispensary managers, key budtenders
  • Share content with partner dispensary for their own channels
T+2 to T+6 Weeks
The Long Tail
  • Weekly social posts from event content library — product shots, behind-the-scenes, testimonial clips
  • Feature budtender testimonials in HeadChange newsletter with their name + dispensary
  • Use event photos in sales materials and future event promotion
  • Build a "Sesh Recap" blog post or brand archive page on the website
  • Brief sales team on dispensary contacts captured — hand off for account follow-up

Post-Sesh Email Sequence

Email 1
24–48 hrs

The Thank You

Personal, warm. Acknowledges them by name if possible. "Last night was something special — thanks for being part of it." Include 2–3 photos from the event. No hard sell.

Email 2
5–7 days

The Recap + Education

Share the full event photo gallery. Include a short piece of educational content tied to what was served — concentrate profiles, temperature guide, or a "How To Hash" excerpt. Positions HeadChange as a knowledge brand.

Email 3
2–3 weeks

The Ask

"Where can you find HeadChange in your store?" or "Share your feedback — what would make the next Sesh even better?" Soft call-to-action that opens a conversation and surfaces sales opportunities.

Ongoing

Monthly Newsletter — Sesh Alumni Segment

All Sesh attendees move into the HeadChange newsletter segment. They get: new drop announcements, next Sesh invitations, educational content, and the occasional exclusive offer. This is the long game — building a true community of industry insiders.

Hot-Swap Protocol Reference

  • DC Coils stay ON — constant heat maintained
  • Banger held at 500/600°F in coil
  • Post-dab: dry-swab then wet-swab immediately
  • Carbon buildup → swap to fresh banger from kit
  • Dirty glass into deep ISO dunk tank to reset
  • Guest always sees a pristine station — zero downtime
