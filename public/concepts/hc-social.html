North Star

Brand Awareness First

Before followers. Before conversions. Before virality. The goal right now is singular: make Head Change the name people associate with quality, craft, and concentrate culture in Missouri — and eventually everywhere.

"For the heads, but welcoming to the curious."

Head Change brand positioning
What we're building
A recognizable visual identity and voice on Instagram that signals craft and authority to anyone who encounters HC for the first time.
What we're not doing
Chasing trends, posting daily for the sake of it, using hype language, or treating Instagram like an ad channel. Quality over quantity, always.
How we measure it
Profile visits, reach, saves, shares, and follower growth. Not likes. The audience we want saves and sends — they don't just double-tap.
Universal CTA

Every Post. Every Time.

No matter what pillar a post belongs to — process, education, culture, or community — every single piece of HC content ends with the same quiet invitation: join the Hash Club.

The Standing Call to Action
"Join the Hash Club. Link in bio."
Every caption. Every Reel description. Every Story swipe-up. The Hash Club (Hash Notes loyalty program) is the destination — social is the on-ramp.
Why it goes on everything
Every post is a first impression for someone. The CTA converts curious scrollers into Hash Notes members — the only channel HC fully owns. Don't waste the impression.
How it stays on-brand
One line. Never pushy. Always at the end, after the content earns it. The CTA is a whisper — not a shout. The post does the work. The CTA just opens the door.
What it leads to
Hash Notes sign-up → scan products → earn points → redeem at webstore. One CTA, the entire loyalty ecosystem behind it. Social feeds the machine.

Production Team

Who's Making This

Two partners. Two distinct roles. One cohesive visual language. Studio Linear handles campaign strategy and creative direction. Local videography handles on-the-ground production.

Campaign Strategy & Creative Direction
Studio Linear
Studio Linear leads campaign ideation, creative strategy, and overall visual direction for Head Change's social presence. They bring the framework — we bring the culture. Together they build campaigns that feel authentic to the scene while reaching beyond it.
Campaign Strategy
Monthly and quarterly campaign themes, content calendars, and pillar assignments aligned with product launches, events, and culture moments.
Creative Direction
Visual mood, aesthetic briefs, shot lists, and art direction for all major content shoots. Ensures grid cohesion and brand consistency.
Content Review & QC
Reviews all content before publishing. Final say on whether something is on-brand, on-tone, and ready to post.
Analytics & Reporting
Monthly performance reports with reach, saves, profile visits, and follower growth. Adjusts strategy accordingly.
On-the-Ground Production
Local Videography
A local videography partner embedded in Missouri's culture — someone who understands the scene, can move quickly, and captures authentic moments without making them feel produced. This is not a commercial video crew. This is a storyteller with a lens.
Video Production
Short-form Reels, process documentation, ambient clips, slow-motion product footage, and event coverage at quarterly sesh events.
Interview Capture
Films hash maker interviews, founder story content, and culture-adjacent features. Unpolished, honest, real.
Event Coverage
Full content day at every quarterly sesh. Photo + video. Raw footage delivered to Studio Linear for editing and repurposing.
Product Macro Shots
Close-up product photography and video — trichomes, melt, texture, color. The visual language of concentrate culture.
Collaboration model: Studio Linear sets the creative brief. Local videography executes the shoot. Raw assets return to Studio Linear for editing, captioning, and scheduling. HC approves before anything goes live. Every piece of content passes through this pipeline.

Voice & Tone

How HC Sounds

The voice is connoisseur authority without clinical distance. Sensory, precise, confident. Still human. Still Missouri.

We do this
Use sensory, texture-forward language — "terpy," "melt," "clean finish"
Let the product and the people speak — minimal copy on strong visuals
Write captions that feel like one head talking to another
Acknowledge the craft, the process, the obsession behind the product
Use silence and space — white space in design, pauses in video
Lean into Missouri pride without making it a gimmick
End every single post with "Join the Hash Club → link in bio"
We don't do this
Use hype words — "fire," "🔥🔥🔥," "game changer," "slaps"
Post daily for the sake of the algorithm
Sound like a dispensary ad or a brand account on autopilot
Explain things in a condescending way to new users
Chase audio trends or meme formats that don't fit the aesthetic
Mix in unrelated culture content just to seem relatable

Measurement

What We Track

Brand awareness is the goal right now — so we track metrics that tell us if people are noticing, remembering, and coming back. Not vanity numbers.

👁
Reach
Total unique accounts who see HC content each month. Primary awareness signal. Are we getting in front of new people?
Watch for: month-over-month growth
🔖
Saves
Saves signal that content is genuinely valuable — especially education posts and product shots. High saves = HC is a reference, not just a scroll.
Best indicator of: educational content quality
📤
Shares / Sends
When someone shares an HC post, that's endorsement — especially in the concentrate community. Memes and education posts drive this most.
Best indicator of: cultural resonance
👤
Profile Visits
Are people curious enough to click through after seeing a post? Profile visits tell us if content is making people want to know more about HC.
Tracked alongside: follower conversion rate
Monthly reporting: Studio Linear delivers a one-page performance snapshot at the start of each month covering the prior month's reach, saves, shares, follower growth, and top-performing content. HC uses this to inform which pillars to double down on and where to adjust.
